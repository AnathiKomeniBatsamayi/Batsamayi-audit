Introduction

Bespoke Auditing is your trusted audit and advisory service provider, In today's rapidly evolving technological landscape, businesses face ever-increasing challenges in safeguarding their digital assets, ensuring compliance with regulations, and optimising their IT infrastructure for maximum efficiency and security. Trust Bespoke Audit to understand the importance of ensuring the security, efficiency, and compliance of your organisation's IT infrastructure. Our team of experts is dedicated to delivering customised auditing and advisory services designed to meet the needs and challenges of your organisation.

Comprehensive IT Audit Services

General Controls Audits

We evaluate the overarching controls in place to ensure the reliability, integrity, and availability of IT systems. This includes reviews of access controls, change management, data backups, and disaster recovery procedures.

Application Controls Audits

Our team examines specific applications to ensure that controls are in place to guarantee data accuracy, validity, and completeness. This involves testing input, processing, and output controls, as well as application-level security measures.

Network Security Audits

We assess the security of network infrastructures, identifying vulnerabilities and recommending measures to protect against threats. This includes penetration testing, firewall reviews, and intrusion detection system evaluations.

Compliance Audits

We ensure that our clients are adhering to relevant regulations and standards, including POPIA, PAIA, ECTA and more. Our audits help clients avoid legal penalties and reputational damage by maintaining compliance with industry and government mandates.

Operational Audits

Our operational audits focus on the efficiency and effectiveness of IT operations. We identify areas for improvement in IT processes and practices, helping clients optimise their IT resources and enhance overall performance.
